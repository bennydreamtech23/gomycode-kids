About Us

We train the next tech generation on the most innovative and recent technologies. We help them get ready for the jobs of the future and prepare them for their professional endeavour while reinforcing their employability. With these new Programme for kid added we want to also carry the kid to the future and show them to take part in the building of the future.

The "Kids" program is specially designed for children passionate about computers, new technologies or robotics. It is designed to allow children to learn the skills necessary to create their technological application while having fun and interacting with members of their group.
